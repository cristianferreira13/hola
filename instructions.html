Instructions and Formations

Learn how to play FIFA like a pro with our expert guides and formations!

Basic Controls

  • Left Stick: Movement
  • Right Stick: Skill Moves
  • X/A: Pass/Header
  • Square/X: Shoot/Volley/Header
  • Circle/B: Lob Pass/Cross/Header
  • Triangle/Y: Through Pass/Chip Shot/Header
  • L1/LB: Player Run/Modifier
  • R1/RB: Sprint/Modifier
  • R2/RT: Skill Moves Modifier
  • L2/LT: Close Control/Modifier

Formations

Here are some popular formations used by top FIFA players:

  • 4-2-3-1: A solid formation with a strong defensive midfield and plenty of attacking options.
  • 4-3-3: A balanced formation with three central midfielders and wide attackers providing width.
  • 4-1-2-1-2: A narrow formation with two strikers and a central attacking midfielder creating chances.
